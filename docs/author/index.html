Greg Wilson has worked in industry and academia for over 40 years and is the author, co-author, or editor of several books, including Beautiful Code, The Architecture of Open Source Applications, JavaScript for Data Science, Teaching Tech Together, and Research Software Engineering with Python. He was the co-founder and first Executive Director of Software Carpentry and received ACM SIGSOFT’s Influential Educator Award in 2020.
